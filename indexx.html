10th Country Programme Evaluation Workshop

Workshop Overview

This interactive platform provides an overview of the recommendations from the Country Programme Evaluation (CPE) for the UNFPA Bangladesh 10th Country Programme (2022-2026). The goal is to develop and refine strong, actionable recommendations grounded in the evaluation report's conclusions. Your input is vital to ensure these recommendations have a meaningful and practical impact on our future programme design and delivery.
