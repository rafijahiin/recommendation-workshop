10th Country Programme Evaluation

Recommendation Workshop

Welcome to the Workshop

This workshop is a crucial step in our evaluation process. As outlined in the UNFPA Evaluation Handbook 2024, evaluations serve three main purposes:

  • Accountability: Demonstrating our performance and use of resources to stakeholders.
  • Decision-making: Supporting evidence-based choices for future programming.
  • Organizational Learning: Identifying lessons to improve how we achieve our goals.

The following recommendations have been carefully developed from the evaluation's findings and conclusions. Your feedback is vital to refine these recommendations, ensuring they are actionable, relevant, and impactful for the next country programme cycle.

Please review each recommendation and use the feedback button to share your valuable insights.

Thank You

Thank you for your active participation and valuable contributions to this workshop.

Your feedback is instrumental in shaping the future of UNFPA's work in Bangladesh and ensuring we continue to deliver a world where every pregnancy is wanted, every childbirth is safe, and every young person's potential is fulfilled.

The final evaluation report, incorporating the outcomes of this workshop, will be shared in due course.
