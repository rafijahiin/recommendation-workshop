Report – Purpose & Scope

Purpose & Objectives

  • Accountability on results and use of resources.
  • Evidence for decision-making across the HDP nexus.
  • Organizational learning on how to achieve results.
  • Inform design of the next CPD; current CP extended to Dec 2026.

(See: Purpose/objectives, audience, and CP extension.)

Scope

  • Temporal: 2022 – mid‑July 2025; forward‑looking to 2026.
  • Geographic: National + priority districts + Cox’s Bazar.
  • Thematic: SRHR; GEWE/GBV; Adolescents & Youth; Population Dynamics. Cross‑cutting: LNOB, disability inclusion, innovation, HDP nexus.

Approach & Methods

  • Theory‑based evaluation anchored in four thematic ToCs; contribution analysis.
  • Participatory, rights‑based, LNOB lens (KIIs, FGDs, observations).
  • Mixed methods with secondary quantitative data; triangulation.
  • Ethics and safeguards for vulnerable groups; consent procedures.

Recommendations – Overview

Six core recommendations. Use Jump To to open any card directly. Feedback is collected once via the single form (top/right & footer).

  1. Umbrella ToC (High): unify, risk‑inform, and align AWPs/budgets; joint governance; minimal dashboard.
  2. Scale Proven Models (High): standardize, budgetize, codify; QA & fidelity; SSTC replication kits; adoption tracking.
  3. Effective LNOB (High): visible equity via a lean indicator pack, targets/floors, safeguards, and finance link.
  4. Continuity & Efficiency (High): one delivery calendar; SLAs; forecasting; last‑mile basics; ops dashboard.
  5. Funding → Financing (High): MoF/ERD compact; cost & code; pooled procurement; equity finance; bridge the transition.
  6. Strategic Innovation (Med‑High): protocol & registry; inclusion/safety gates; budget/indicator enablement; scale dashboard.

Recommendations Workshop Protocol

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Evaluation Team: ensure evidence‑based findings, conclusions, and draft recommendations that derive from conclusions.
  • ERG: refine wording, feasibility, and operational implications; confirm priority & addressees.

Use the worksheet structure to capture rationale, priority, addressee(s), and implications.

Process

  • ERG reviews draft final report, then attends half‑day workshop.
  • Each recommendation: review → refine wording → agree on operational implications.
  • Track changes and finalize a clean set for management response.
